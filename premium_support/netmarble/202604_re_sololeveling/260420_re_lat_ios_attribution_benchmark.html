Key Findings

  • 72.3% of all iOS RE LAT postbacks are deterministic (deeplink-based) — only 27.7% probabilistic. Probabilistic is a minority, not the default.
  • KOR office advertisers are significantly more reliable: 94.5% deterministic over 6 months — the deeplink infrastructure is robust across the KOR portfolio.
  • April 2026 shows a KOR deterministic dip to 86.6% (vs. 96%+ historically), driven by new probabilistic campaigns entering the mix (Lucky Defense, Hwahae). Not a platform regression.
  • Probabilistic attribution is only used as a fallback when no deeplink is available — it does not replace or contaminate deterministic signals. Netmarble's concern about data integrity is not supported by the data.
